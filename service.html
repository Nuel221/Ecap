Our Skills

At Youth and Adolescent Empowerment (YAE), we offer a diverse range of vocational and digital skills training tailored to help young people discover their passions, build practical experience, and unlock real-world opportunities. Each of our services is designed to be hands-on, inclusive, and impactful, empowering youth with the confidence and ability to thrive in today’s competitive world.

Tailoring and Fashion Design

We equip learners with the skills to design, cut, sew, and finish garments. From basic stitching to advanced fashion design techniques, participants gain the creativity and expertise to launch their own clothing brands or work in the fashion industry.

Tie and Dye (Adire)

Our traditional and modern tie and dye classes teach the art of fabric design using local materials. This craft encourages cultural creativity and offers a path to entrepreneurship through handmade textile production.

Catering and Pastries

We train aspiring chefs and bakers in the art of cooking, baking, and presentation. Whether it’s catering for events or opening a food business, our program covers recipes, food safety, menu planning, and kitchen management.

Makeup and Hairdressing

This beauty-focused program introduces learners to professional makeup techniques, hair styling, and salon management. It’s perfect for those wanting to work in the beauty industry or offer freelance services.

Interior Decoration and Design

Participants learn the fundamentals of space planning, color coordination, home aesthetics, and event decoration. This course blends creativity with business by opening doors to event planning and interior styling careers.

Coding and Digital Skills

From website development to basic programming, this tech-forward service empowers youth with digital literacy and coding abilities. Participants are introduced to the world of software, apps, and online freelancing—opening doors to global job opportunities.

Empowering the Youths and Adolecents to use their own hands to achieve their dreams and have a better tomorrow.
