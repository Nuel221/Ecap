About YAE

Empowering the Next Generation to unlock their potential with hands-on vocational trainingand to become the entrepreneur or professional you aspire to be.

Empowering Youth,

Unlocking Opportunities

At Youth and Adolescent Empowerment (YAE), we believe that every young person holds the potential to create a meaningful future — all they need is the right opportunity, guidance, and tools. YAE is a dynamic empowerment initiative dedicated to equipping youth and adolescents with practical vocational skills that lead to independence, entrepreneurship, and career success. We provide hands-on training in a range of marketable skills including tailoring, catering and pastries, tie & dye, makeup and hairdressing, interior design, and digital skills like coding.

Our programs are designed not only to teach, but to inspire creativity, build confidence, and nurture the entrepreneurial spirit in every participant. We focus on practical learning, mentorship, and community impact — ensuring that each young person who walks through our doors walks out ready to face the world with purpose. Since our inception, YAE has empowered hundreds of youth from various backgrounds — many of whom are now running their own businesses, earning steady incomes, or continuing their education with a renewed sense of direction.

Whether you're a young person eager to learn, a parent seeking growth opportunities for your child, or a partner looking to make a difference, YAE welcomes you to join our mission of empowerment, transformation, and hope.

Together, we can build a brighter, skill-driven future for our youth.

VISION

To build a generation of skilled, confident, and innovative young leaders who positively impact their communities and the world. To create a generation of skilled youth leading the way in entrepreneurship, creativity, and technology.

MISSION

To empower young individuals by providing them with hands-on vocational skills that inspire innovation, self-reliance, and community impact. To inspire and empower young people by equipping them with practical skills and resources that foster entrepreneurship, creativity, and self-reliance in a dynamic and ever-changing world.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in the transformative power of education and skills.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and out-of-the-box thinking in all our programs.
  • Inclusivity: Providing equal opportunities to all youth, regardless of their background.
  • Integrity: Maintaining transparency and ethical practices in everything we do.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in training and mentorship.
