Welcome to Youth and Adolescent Empowerment (YEA)

At Youth and Adolescent Empowerment (YEA), we believe every young person holds the potential to create a brighter future—for themselves, their families, and their communities. Our mission is to unlock that potential by providing opportunities to learn practical, life-changing vocational skills and fostering an environment that nurtures creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship. From tailoring and fashion design to coding and digital skills, our programs are tailored to equip youth and adolescents with the tools they need to thrive in today’s competitive world. Whether you dream of starting your own business, advancing your career, or simply learning a new craft, YEA is here to guide you every step of the way. Join us as we empower a generation of skilled, confident, and self-reliant young individuals. Together, we can shape a world of possibilities.

ABOUT US

At Youth and Adolescent Empowerment, we believe in the power of skills to transform lives. Our platform offers a supportive and inclusive environment where youth and adolescents can learn vocational skills that prepare them for independence and success in today’s world.

GOAL

At Youth and Adolescent Empowerment (YAE), our goal is to equip young people with the skills, confidence and opportunities they need to build successful careers, launch their own businesses, and become self-reliant members of the society.

To bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by making vocational education accessible, affordable, and practical.

MISSION

To empower young individuals by providing them with hands-on vocational skills that inspire innovation, self-reliance, and community impact.

To inspire and empower young people by equipping them with practical skills and resources that foster entrepreneurship, creativity, and self-reliance in a dynamic and ever-changing world.

VISION

To build a generation of skilled, confident, and innovative young leaders who positively impact their communities and the world.

To create a generation of skilled youth leading the way in entrepreneurship, creativity, and technology.
